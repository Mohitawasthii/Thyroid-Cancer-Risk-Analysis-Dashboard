## 📌 Project Overview

The **Thyroid Cancer Risk Analysis Dashboard** is an interactive **Power BI** project designed to analyze thyroid cancer patient data and identify key risk factors influencing diagnosis. The dashboard provides insights into patient demographics, diagnosis status, thyroid cancer risk levels, lifestyle factors, geographic distribution, and clinical indicators to support healthcare decision-making.

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze thyroid cancer patient data.
- Identify high, medium, and low-risk patient groups.
- Compare benign and malignant diagnoses.
- Explore demographic and geographic trends.
- Monitor key clinical indicators.
- Build an interactive healthcare dashboard for data-driven insights.

## 📂 Dataset Features

The dataset includes:

- Patient ID
- Age
- Gender
- Country
- Diagnosis
- Thyroid Cancer Risk
- Smoking Status
- Radiation Exposure
- TSH Level
- T3 Level
- Age Group

## 📈 Key Insights

- Over half of the patients fall into the **Low Risk** category.
- Benign cases account for the majority of diagnoses.
- Older age groups (60+) show the highest patient count.
- Female patients represent a larger share of thyroid-related cases.
- Patient distribution varies significantly across countries.
- Clinical indicators such as TSH and T3 levels provide valuable insights for monitoring thyroid health.
